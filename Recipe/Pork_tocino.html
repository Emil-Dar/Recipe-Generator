Pork Tocino (Sweet Cured Pork)

Meal Time

Breakfast

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork loin
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Prague powder (for curing)

Alternative:

  • Pork belly (cut into strips)
  • Brown sugar
  • Achuete oil (for color, optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions (Both):

1. Combine brown sugar, salt, pepper, and achuete oil (if using) to create a rub.
2. Rub the mixture onto the pork.
3. Marinate for at least 8 hours, or preferably overnight.
4. Pan-fry the pork strips until cooked through and slightly caramelized.
