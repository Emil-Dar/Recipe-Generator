Leche Flan (Steamed Custard)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Alternative:

  • Eggs
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions (Both):

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, evaporated milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
3. Caramel (optional): In a separate pan, melt sugar over low heat until golden brown. Pour the caramel into a mold or individual ramekins.
4. Carefully pour the egg mixture over the caramel (if using).
5. Place the mold or ramekins in a baking pan filled with water (bain-marie).
6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
7. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
