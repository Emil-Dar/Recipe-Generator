Pinakbet (Vegetable Stew)

Meal Time

Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Baguio beans
  • Okra
  • Eggplant
  • Squash
  • Tomatoes
  • Shrimp paste
  • Pork belly

Alternative:

  • Kangkong (water spinach)
  • Green beans
  • Chayote (chayote squash)
  • Bitter melon
  • Tomatoes
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Ground pork

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
3. Pour water and bring to a boil.
4. Add the vegetables (depending on your choice) and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
5. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
