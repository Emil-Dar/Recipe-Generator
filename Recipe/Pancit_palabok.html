Pansit Palabok (Filipino Noodle Dish)

Meal Time

Snack; Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork belly
  • Shrimp
  • Tinapay (dried shrimp flakes)
  • Carrots
  • Sayote (chayote squash)
  • Shrimp paste
  • Pork rinds
  • Palabok noodles

Alternative:

  • Ground chicken or pork
  • Hipon (small shrimp)
  • Achuete oil (for coloring)
  • Carrots
  • Cabbage
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Fried tofu (instead of pork rinds)
  • Bihon noodles (rice noodles)

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add your chosen meat and cook until browned.
3. Add water and bring to a boil.
4. Simmer the broth for 30 minutes.
5. In a separate pan, dissolve achuete oil (or paprika) in water to create an orange sauce.
6. Strain the broth and add the orange sauce for coloring.
7. Cook the bihon noodles (or palabok noodles) according to package instructions.
8. Drain and set aside.
9. Assemble the dish by layering noodles, cooked vegetables, meat, fried tofu, and a boiled egg (optional).
