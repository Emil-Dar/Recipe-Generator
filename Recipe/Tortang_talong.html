Tortang Talong (Eggplant Omelette)

Meal Time

Breakfast

Ingredients

Original:

  • Eggplant
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Shrimp paste

Alternative:

  • Malunggay leaves (moringa leaves) instead of eggplant
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Patis (fish sauce)

Instructions (Both):

1. Grill or fry eggplant (or malunggay leaves) until tender. Set aside.
2. Saut√© onions and tomatoes until softened.
3. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt.
4. Add the cooked vegetables to the beaten eggs.
5. Heat oil in a pan and pour the egg mixture.
6. Cook until the bottom is set, then flip and cook until the other side is cooked through.
