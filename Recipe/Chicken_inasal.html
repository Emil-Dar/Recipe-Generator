Chicken Inasal (Grilled Chicken)

Meal Time

Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Chicken leg quarters
  • Achuete oil
  • Lemon grass
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Coconut milk
  • Vinegar
  • Chilies

Alternative:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Paprika (instead of achuete oil)
  • Lemongrass (optional)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Coconut milk
  • Calamansi (or lemon juice)
  • Chilies (optional)

Instructions (Both):

1. Combine all marinade ingredients (except coconut milk) in a bowl.
2. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight for deeper flavor.
3. Grill the chicken over charcoal or pan-fry until cooked through.
4. (Optional) Reduce the remaining marinade with coconut milk to create a dipping sauce.
