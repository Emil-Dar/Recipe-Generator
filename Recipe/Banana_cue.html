Banana Cue (Fried Bananas coated in Caramel)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Original:

  • Saba bananas (plantains)
  • Brown sugar
  • Water
  • Sesame seeds

Alternative:

  • Ripe bananas
  • White sugar
  • Water
  • Peanuts (chopped)

Instructions (Both):

1. Peel the bananas and cut them into diagonal slices.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Fry the bananas until golden brown.
3. In a separate pan, combine sugar and water. Cook until sugar dissolves and thickens slightly, forming a caramel syrup.
4. Dip the fried bananas in the caramel sauce and coat evenly.
5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds (or chopped peanuts) before serving.
