Pinakbet Bagnet (Pork Belly with Vegetables)

Meal Time

Breakfast; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork belly (bagnet)
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste)
  • Vegetables (okra, string beans, eggplant, squash)

Alternative:

  • Pork shoulder (cut into cubes)
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Vegetables (malunggay leaves, kangkong, chayote squash, bitter melon)

Instructions (Both):

1. Deep fry pork belly (or cube and pan-fry pork shoulder) until crispy. Set aside.
2. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
3. Add chosen vegetables and cook until tender-crisp.
4. Pour water or broth and bring to a boil.
5. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
6. Serve pork belly (or pork shoulder) on top of the vegetables with the flavorful broth.
