Lumpia Shanghai (Spring Rolls)

Meal Time

Snacks; Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Ground pork
  • Chopped onions, carrots, green peas, shrimps
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Egg (for sealing)

Alternative:

  • Ground chicken or turkey
  • Chopped cabbage, bell peppers, mushrooms instead of vegetables
  • Lumpia wrapper substitutes (wonton wrappers, rice paper)

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© meat, onions, and vegetables until cooked.
2. Season with soy sauce, pepper, and garlic.
3. Place a spoonful of filling on a wrapper, roll tightly, and seal with egg wash.
4. Fry until golden brown.
