Bicol Express (Spicy Pork with Coconut Milk)

Meal Time

Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork belly
  • Chilies (finger chilies or chilies in oil)
  • Shrimp paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions

Alternative:

  • Ground pork
  • Chili flakes (or hot sauce)
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Coconut milk
  • Green beans
  • Onions

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add chosen pork and cook until browned.
3. Add chilies (or chili flakes/hot sauce) according to desired spice level.
4. Pour coconut milk and bring to a boil.
5. Add vegetables and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until tender.
6. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
