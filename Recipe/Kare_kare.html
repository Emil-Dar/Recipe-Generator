Kare-Kare (Oxtail Stew)

Meal Time

Breakfast; Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Oxtail
  • Peanut butter, bagoong (shrimp paste)
  • Vegetables (eggplant, string beans, pechay)
  • Banana blossoms

Alternative:

  • Pork belly or tripe (cut into pieces)
  • Ground peanuts instead of peanut butter
  • Patis (fish sauce) instead of bagoong

Instructions (Both):

1. Boil oxtail (or substitute) until tender. Set aside.
2. Saut√© onions, garlic, and bagoong (or patis).
3. Add peanut butter (or ground peanuts) and mix well.
4. Pour water or broth and vegetables. Simmer until vegetables soften.
5. Add oxtail (or substitute) and simmer for a few minutes.
