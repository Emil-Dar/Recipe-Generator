Adobo (Chicken/Pork)

Meal Time

Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Chicken or pork (cut into pieces)
  • Soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, bay leaves

Alternative:

  • Chicken legs or thighs (bone-in)
  • Coconut amino instead of soy sauce
  • Patis (fish sauce) instead of vinegar

Instructions (Both):

1. Combine meat, soy sauce (or coconut amino), vinegar (or patis), garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves in a pot.
2. Add water and bring to a boil.
3. Lower heat and simmer until meat is tender and sauce thickens.
