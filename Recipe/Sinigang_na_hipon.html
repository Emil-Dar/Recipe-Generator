Sinigang na Hipon (Shrimp Sinigang)

Meal Time

Breakfast

Ingredients

Original:

  • Large shrimp (cleaned)
  • Tamarind
  • Onions, tomatoes, okra, green beans
  • Shrimp paste, fish sauce, salt, pepper

Alternative:

  • Smaller, cheaper shrimp (like hipon)
  • Guava or green mangoes instead of tamarind
  • Malunggay leaves or kangkong instead of okra

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions, garlic, and tomatoes.
2. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
3. Pour water and bring to a boil.
4. Add tamarind (or guava/mango) and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Add vegetables and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
