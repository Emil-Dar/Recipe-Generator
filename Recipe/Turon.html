Turon (Banana Spring Rolls)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Original:

  • Saba bananas (plantains)
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil

Alternative:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Spring roll wrappers (or wonton wrappers)
  • Brown sugar
  • Margarine

Instructions (Both):

1. Peel and slice bananas into desired thickness.
2. Sprinkle with sugar (or brown sugar) for added sweetness (optional).
3. Wrap the banana slices in a spring roll wrapper (or wonton wrapper).
4. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
5. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
