Ginataang Gulay (Vegetables in Coconut Milk)

Meal Time

Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Shrimp
  • Kangkong (water spinach)
  • String beans
  • Okra
  • Coconut milk
  • Shrimp paste

Alternative:

  • Tofu
  • Calabaza (squash)
  • Sitaw (yardlong beans)
  • Pechay (bok choy)
  • Coconut milk
  • Patis (fish sauce)

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add your chosen vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
3. Pour coconut milk and bring to a boil.
4. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
5. Simmer for 5-7 minutes or until vegetables are cooked through.
6. (Optional) Add tofu and simmer for another 2 minutes.
