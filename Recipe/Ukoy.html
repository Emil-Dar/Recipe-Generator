Ukoy (Shrimp Fritters)

Meal Time

Breakfast; Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Shrimp
  • Onions
  • Green beans
  • Squash
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Patis (fish sauce)

Alternative:

  • Sardines (mashed)
  • Chopped cabbage
  • Bell peppers (optional)
  • Saba banana (grated)
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions (Both):

1. Combine chosen protein (shrimp or mashed sardines), chopped vegetables, flour, eggs, and seasoning.
2. Mix well to form a thick batter.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Scoop spoonfuls of batter and fry until golden brown and cooked through.
5. Drain on paper towels and serve with dipping sauce (optional).
