Bistek Tagalog (Filipino Beef Steak)

Meal Time

Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Beef sirloin
  • Soy sauce
  • Calamansi juice
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Butter or oil

Alternative:

  • Beef chuck eye steak (marinated longer for tenderness)
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil

Instructions (Both):

1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, calamansi juice (or vinegar), onions, and garlic for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight.
2. Heat oil in a pan and sear the beef on both sides until desired doneness.
3. Set aside the beef and cook the leftover marinade in the pan until slightly reduced.
4. Slice the beef and serve with the sauce.
