Ginataang Mais (Corn in Coconut Milk)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Original:

  • Fresh corn kernels
  • Shrimp
  • Coconut milk
  • Shrimp paste
  • Vegetables (okra, string beans)

Alternative:

  • Frozen corn kernels
  • Cubed chicken or pork
  • Coconut milk
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Alugbati (malabar spinach) or kangkong (water spinach)

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add your chosen meat (if using) and cook until browned.
3. Pour coconut milk and bring to a boil.
4. Add corn kernels and simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add chosen vegetables and simmer for another 2-3 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
6. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
