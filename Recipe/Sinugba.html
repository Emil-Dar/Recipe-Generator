Sinugba (Grilled Seafood Skewers)

Meal Time

Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Assorted seafood (squid, shrimp, scallops)
  • Banana blossom (optional)
  • Achuete oil
  • Lemon grass
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Chilies

Alternative:

  • Tilapia or bangus (milkfish) cut into cubes
  • Eggplant slices (instead of banana blossom)
  • Paprika (instead of achuete oil)
  • Lemongrass (optional)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Calamansi (or lemon juice)
  • Chilies (optional)

Instructions (Both):

1. Combine marinade ingredients (except vinegar). Marinate seafood for at least 30 minutes.
2. Prepare skewers by threading marinated seafood and vegetables (if using).
3. Grill over charcoal or pan-fry until cooked through.
4. Serve with vinegar or a dipping sauce of your choice.
