Sinigang na Baboy (Pork Sinigang)

Meal Time

Breakfast; Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork belly
  • Tamarind
  • Onions, tomatoes, okra, green beans
  • Shrimp paste, fish sauce, salt, pepper

Alternative:

  • Boneless pork cuts (shoulder, loin)
  • Guava or green mangoes instead of tamarind
  • Malunggay leaves or kangkong instead of okra

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions, garlic, and tomatoes.
2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
3. Pour water and bring to a boil.
4. Add tamarind (or guava/mango) and simmer for 30 minutes.
5. Add vegetables and simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
