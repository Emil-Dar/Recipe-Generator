Buko Pandan (Coconut Pandan Jelly)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Original:

  • Young coconut meat
  • Pandan leaves
  • Sugar
  • Agar-agar powder
  • Water

Alternative:

  • Mature coconut meat (grated)
  • Pandan leaves
  • Brown sugar
  • Gelatin powder
  • Water

Instructions (Both):

1. Boil water and pandan leaves for a few minutes to extract flavor.
2. Strain the pandan-flavored water.
3. Add sugar (or brown sugar) and agar-agar powder (or gelatin) to the pandan water.
4. Heat until sugar dissolves and agar-agar/gelatin dissolves and thickens slightly.
5. Add shredded coconut meat and stir well.
6. Pour mixture into a mold and refrigerate until set.
