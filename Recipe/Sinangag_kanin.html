Sinangag na Kanin (Garlic Fried Rice)

Meal Time

Breakfast; Lunch; Dinner

Ingredients

Original:

  • Leftover cooked rice
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salted eggs
  • Longganisa (sausages)

Alternative:

  • Cold cooked rice
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salted fish
  • Leftover pork or chicken scraps (optional)

Instructions (Both):

1. Heat oil in a pan. Saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
3. Add leftover rice and mash slightly with a spatula.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. (Optional) Add crumbled salted eggs or flaked fish, and cook for another minute.
6. (Optional) Add leftover cooked pork or chicken for additional protein.
