Pinakbet na Sardines (Sardines with Vegetables)

Meal Time

Lunch

Ingredients

Original:

  • Pork belly
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste)
  • Vegetables (okra, string beans, eggplant, squash)

Alternative:

  • Canned sardines in tomato sauce
  • Patis (fish sauce)
  • Vegetables (malunggay leaves, kangkong, chayote squash, bitter melon)

Instructions (Both):

1. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add chosen vegetables and cook until tender-crisp.
3. Pour water or broth and bring to a boil.
4. Add canned sardines and mash slightly to release the flavor.
5. Season with patis (fish sauce) and salt to taste.
