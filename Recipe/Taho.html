Taho (Silken Tofu with Sago Pearls)

Meal Time

Snacks

Ingredients

Original:

  • Silken tofu
  • Sago pearls
  • Arnibal (sweetened syrup)
  • Tapioca pearls (optional)

Alternative:

  • Firm tofu (crumbled)
  • Gulaman (agar-agar jelly cubes)
  • Brown sugar syrup
  • Sago pearls (optional)

Instructions (Both):

1. Cook sago pearls (or tapioca pearls) according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. For the arnibal (or brown sugar syrup): Combine water and brown sugar in a saucepan. Heat until sugar dissolves. Add a pinch of vanilla extract (optional).
3. In a bowl, combine cubed tofu (or silken tofu), sago pearls (or tapioca pearls), and syrup.
4. Serve chilled.
