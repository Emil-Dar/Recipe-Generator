Atchara (Pickled Papaya)

Meal Time

Snacks/Flavorings

Ingredients

Original:

  • Unripe papaya
  • Carrots
  • Raisins
  • Bell peppers
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Alternative:

  • Green mangoes (instead of papaya)
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Raisins (optional)
  • Onions
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Instructions (Both):

1. Julienne papaya (or mangoes) and carrots (if using).
2. Combine vegetables, vinegar, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
3. Let sit for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to develop.
4. (Optional) Add raisins for a touch of sweetness.
